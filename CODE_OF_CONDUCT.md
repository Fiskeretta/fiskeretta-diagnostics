# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Fiskeretta to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience, background, or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps:

- Being respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and discussions.
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get oriented.
- Gracefully accepting and giving constructive feedback.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, trolling, or
publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate
this code, and may block repeat offenders. To report a concern privately, use
the **"Report a vulnerability"** button on the repository's **Security** tab, or
contact a maintainer directly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
