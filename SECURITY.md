# Security & Safety

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please **don't** open a public issue for security problems. Instead use GitHub's
**"Report a vulnerability"** button on the repository's **Security** tab — that
opens a private report only the maintainers can see. We'll respond as soon as we
can.

For non-sensitive bugs, a normal [issue](../../issues) is the right place.

## Safety note — this is a vehicle tool

Fiskeretta communicates with a car over its OBD2 port. It is provided with **no
warranty** and is used **entirely at your own risk**:

- Only use it on a safely **parked** vehicle — never while driving.
- Clearing DTCs permanently erases the car's stored diagnostic history.
- The authors accept no responsibility for any damage or consequences arising
  from its use.
